Questions

Frequently asked

What is a PWA and how do I install it?
A Progressive Web App (PWA) is a web app you install directly from your browser — no app store needed. On iPhone: open in Safari → tap Share → "Add to Home Screen." On Android: tap the browser menu → "Add to Home Screen." It looks and feels like a native app.
Do I need an internet connection to use it?
The planner itself works offline once installed. The YouVersion links will open the app on your device, which requires a connection for full reading.
Where is my journal data saved?
All your notes and progress are saved locally on your device using browser storage. Your data stays private — it never leaves your device.
Which Bible translation does it use?
Word & Defense is translation-neutral. The YouVersion links open in your preferred translation inside the YouVersion app, which supports hundreds of translations.
What apologetics tradition does it follow?
The insights draw from classical and evidential apologetics — including thinkers like William Lane Craig, C.S. Lewis, and J.P. Moreland — and are broadly evangelical in perspective.
